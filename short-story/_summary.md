# Summary

## Story Blurb
When a failing orbital station sends one hundred young exiles to a hostile Earth, survival quickly turns into a political reckoning. Under Rhea Kane's leadership, rival ground factions, defectors, and former enemies uncover proof that Helios elites have been engineering conflict from above. Through fragile alliances, a dangerous summit broadcast, and costly battles, they expose the truth and force a new social contract. The story ends not with perfect peace, but with a hard-won system built on shared power, accountability, and a founding promise: no one is expendable.

## Name Summary
- Rhea Kane: Central protagonist and coalition leader.
- Dex Mercer: Bold scout who becomes a frontline defender.
- Jonah Hale: Tactical organizer focused on discipline and safeguards.
- Iris Quill: Analyst, medic, and communications strategist.
- Talan: River Clan diplomat and field commander.
- Krell: Ash Wolves leader who shifts from rival to ally.
- Lenn: Former Wolf member whose intelligence changes the war.
- Bo Lin: Mechanic-builder behind critical infrastructure.
- Sera: Apprentice engineer supporting uplink restoration.
- Mira Voss: Helios commander connected to the initial mission.
- Orin Vale: Corrupt councillor driving covert conflict.
- Lysa Renn: Acting chancellor negotiating post-crisis governance.

## Groups and places:
- The Hundred: First exile cohort sent to Earth.
- Orbit Station Helios: Orbital habitat under resource collapse.
- River Clan: Ground faction with strong territorial and civic traditions.
- Ash Wolves: Raider faction manipulated by Helios politics.
- Summit Spire: High tower used for the critical truth broadcast.
- Valley Accord: Foundational power-sharing agreement on Earth.
